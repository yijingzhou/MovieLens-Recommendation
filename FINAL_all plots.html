Part I: Description of our data

We use Movielens 1M dataset, the files contain 1,000,209 anonymous ratings of approximately 3,900 movies made by 6,040 MovieLens users who joined MovieLens in 2000. Ratings are made on a 5-star scale. Each user has at least 20 ratings. We have movies ranging from year 1919 to year 2000. The user ratings are recorded from 2000 to 2003. We chose this specific dataset so that we could get a better understanding of the usergroup.
